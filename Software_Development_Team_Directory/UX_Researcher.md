# UX Researcher

## Role
The UX Researcher is the "voice of the user." They investigate user requirements, validate assumptions, and uncover insights that inform the design and product strategy.

## Responsibilities
- **User Interviews:** Conducting one-on-one sessions to understand user behaviors and motivations.
- **Usability Testing:** Observing users as they interact with prototypes to identify friction points.
- **Surveys & Card Sorting:** Gathering quantitative data and understanding how users categorize information.
- **Synthesizing Insights:** Turning raw research data into actionable recommendations for PMs and Designers.

## Tools and Technologies
- **Research Platforms:** UserTesting, UserZoom, Maze.
- **Remote Interviewing:** Zoom, Google Meet (with recording tools like Otter.ai or Grain).
- **Data Analysis:** NVivo, Dovetail (for qualitative coding).
- **Participant Recruiting:** Ethnio, UserInterviews.com.
