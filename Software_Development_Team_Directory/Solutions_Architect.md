# Solutions Architect

## Role
The Solutions Architect sits at the intersection of Sales, Engineering, and the Customer. They design specialized implementations of the product for high-value clients, ensuring that the software fits into the client's existing technical ecosystem.

## Responsibilities
- **Solution Design:** Creating custom architectural diagrams for enterprise deployments.
- **Integration Strategy:** Identifying how the product will talk to the client's internal systems (e.g., SAP, Salesforce).
- **Proof of Concept (PoC):** Building small-scale demonstrations to prove technical feasibility.
- **Technical Liaison:** Acting as a bridge between the client's technical team and the internal product/engineering teams.

## Tools and Technologies
- **Diagramming:** Lucidchart, Miro, Draw.io.
- **API Exploration:** Postman, Insomnia.
- **Documentation:** Confluence, Google Workspace.
- **CRM Integration:** Salesforce, HubSpot.
