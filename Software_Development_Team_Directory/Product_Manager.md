# Product Manager (PM)

## Role
The Product Manager is the "CEO of the product" (though without the direct authority over people). They are responsible for the overall success of the product, balancing user needs, business goals, and technical feasibility.

## Responsibilities
- **Vision & Roadmap:** Defining the long-term direction and short-term execution plan.
- **Prioritization:** Deciding what to build next based on ROI, user feedback, and strategic alignment.
- **Stakeholder Management:** Aligning marketing, sales, engineering, and executives.
- **Market Research:** Analyzing competitors and identifying new opportunities.
- **User Empathy:** Deeply understanding the pain points of the target audience.

## Tools and Technologies
- **Roadmapping & Issue Tracking:** Jira, Linear, Productboard, Aha!.
- **Product Analytics:** Mixpanel, Amplitude, Heap, Pendo.
- **Feedback Collection:** SurveyMonkey, Typeform, Hotjar.
- **Documentation:** Confluence, Notion, Coda.
- **Prototyping (Review):** Figma (for viewing/commenting on designs).
