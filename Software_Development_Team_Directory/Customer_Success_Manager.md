# Customer Success Manager (CSM)

## Role
CSMs focus on making sure the customer (especially B2B clients) gets actual business value from the product. They are not "sales," but they focus on retention and helping customers adopt new features.

## Responsibilities
- **Onboarding:** Guiding new clients through the setup process.
- **Health Monitoring:** Tracking which features a client is using and identifying those at risk of churn.
- **QBRs (Quarterly Business Reviews):** Meeting with clients to show them the impact the product has on their business.
- **Voice of the Customer:** Funneling high-level feedback back to the Product Manager.

## Tools and Technologies
- **Success Platforms:** Gainsight, Totango, ChurnZero.
- **CRM:** Salesforce, HubSpot.
- **Analytics:** Mixpanel, Pendo (to track user engagement).
- **Communication:** Intercom, Gong (for analyzing client calls).
