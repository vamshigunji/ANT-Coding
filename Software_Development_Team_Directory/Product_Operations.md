# Product Operations (ProdOps)

## Role
Product Operations is the backbone of the product team. They focus on the processes, tools, and data that help PMs and designers do their best work. Their goal is to make the product development lifecycle efficient and scalable.

## Responsibilities
- **Tooling Management:** Selecting and maintaining the product team's software stack (Jira, Figma, etc.).
- **Data Democratization:** Making sure user data and product analytics are accessible and understandable to the whole team.
- **Process Optimization:** Streamlining how feedback moves from Sales/Support to Product.
- **Training & Onboarding:** Teaching new team members the "standard way" of building products at the company.

## Tools and Technologies
- **Workflow Automation:** Zapier, Make (Integromat), Slack Workflows.
- **Knowledge Management:** Guru, Notion, Slite.
- **Product Stack:** Admin access to Pendo, LaunchDarkly (Feature flagging), and Amplitude.
- **CRM Integration:** Salesforce, HubSpot (to connect product data to customer data).
