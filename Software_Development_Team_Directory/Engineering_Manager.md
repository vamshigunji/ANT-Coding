# Engineering Manager (EM)

## Role
The Engineering Manager focuses on the "People" and "Process" side of software development. While they are often former engineers, their primary mission is to ensure their team is healthy, productive, and growing in their careers.

## Responsibilities
- **People Management:** 1-on-1s, career pathing, performance reviews, and resolving interpersonal conflicts.
- **Hiring:** Building the team by identifying talent and designing the interview process.
- **Delivery:** Ensuring the team meets its sprint goals and unblocking technical or bureaucratic hurdles.
- **Strategic Alignment:** Working with PMs to ensure the engineering team's capacity matches the product roadmap.

## Tools and Technologies
- **Performance Management:** Lattice, 15Five, CultureAmp.
- **Agile Management:** Jira, Linear, Shortcut.
- **Resource Planning:** Float, Mavenlink.
- **Communication:** Slack, Tandem, Loom.
