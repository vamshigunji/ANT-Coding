# Technical Product Manager (TPM)

## Role
The Technical Product Manager handles products that are highly technical in nature (e.g., APIs, Cloud Infrastructure, Developer Tools). They bridge the gap between high-level business requirements and deep engineering constraints.

## Responsibilities
- **Technical Specs:** Writing detailed PRDs (Product Requirement Documents) that include API schemas and architectural considerations.
- **Dependency Management:** Coordinating across multiple engineering teams for complex system integrations.
- **Technical Advocacy:** Representing the engineering team's needs (like tech debt reduction) to business stakeholders.
- **Launch Orchestration:** Managing the technical rollout of features, including migrations and deprecations.

## Tools and Technologies
- **API Testing & Documentation:** Postman, Insomnia, Swagger/OpenAPI.
- **System Diagramming:** Lucidchart, Miro, Draw.io.
- **Monitoring & Observability:** Datadog, New Relic (for tracking feature performance).
- **Project Management:** Jira (Advanced Roadmaps), Azure DevOps.
